Different models, different misses — holo 1M13 (ligand HYF)

The pool above is one model (Boltz) with a native, per-target setup — and it hides a 0.59 A pose. Below is the opposite regime: three different architectures (Boltz-1x, Boltz-2, ESMFold-2) co-folding the same holo from a generic PXR sequence + shallow MSA. Every protein backbone aligns to the crystal within 0.5 A (gold cartoon), yet the ligand placements scatter from 3 A to 15 A — and none reach the near-native quality the native-setup pool did. This is the campaign's real lever made visible: input fidelity (target-specific sequence, deep MSA, sampling depth) dominates model choice. It auto-cycles the architectures; the buttons override.
